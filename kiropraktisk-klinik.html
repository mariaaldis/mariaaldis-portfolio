Kiropraktisk
Klinik Sæby

UX/UI Design | Front-end

Kiropraktisk Klink Sæby is a chiropractic center in Northern Denmark. I and three students re-designed their website for a university project. The aim of the project was to create a website that serves as a trustful and professional representation of the clinic and its team.

The Solution

My group and I focused on the information architecture of the website in order to make the website well-stuctured and user-friendly. We made decisions on which content to present based on user-research to ensure content would be relevant to the target audience. User-tests were preformed to ensure users can easily navigate the website.
