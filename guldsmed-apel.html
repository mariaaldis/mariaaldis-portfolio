Guldsmed
Apel

UX/UI Design | Front-end

Guldsmed Apel is a jewlery store located in Copenhagen. Three other students and I re-designed their website for a university project. The goal of the project was to structure the content and present the interface design of the Guldmed Apel's website to enhance its trustworthiness and quality.

The Solution

My group and I focused on the content and the information architecture of the website in order to deliver the store's message in a clear and structured way. We worked on the site's visual identity and reflected the store's luxurious environment through a visual presentation of the information. The result that we obtained is a well-structured, user-friendly and easy to navigate website.
